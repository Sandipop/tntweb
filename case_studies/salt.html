SALT

Salaries that Rewards

Project Summary

SALT was created with a simple yet powerful mission—transforming the way employees access, manage, and benefit from their salaries. Designed for a multi-lingual, global audience, SALT combines salary management with lifestyle benefits in one seamless app. From digital wallets and card management to hotel and flight bookings, the platform empowers users to do more with their money. Its sleek, premium design and intuitive interface ensure users not only manage finances effortlessly but also enjoy exclusive perks and services, reflecting SALT’s vision of “Salaries that Reward.”

The Problem

Many employees and expats struggle to make the most of their salaries. Managing finances, transferring money, and accessing lifestyle services often require multiple apps, resulting in a frustrating and fragmented experience. Traditional banking and salary platforms are rigid, slow, and lack user-friendly design, making it harder for users to take control of their earnings. SALT aims to solve this by unifying essential financial tools with lifestyle perks, enabling users to seamlessly access, manage, and enjoy their salaries in one place.

The Challenges

The biggest challenge was to design an all-in-one salary and lifestyle platform that feels premium yet remains simple for a diverse, multilingual audience. The app had to strike a balance between financial management (wallet, card, transfers) and lifestyle services (hotel and flight booking) without overwhelming users. Ensuring fast, secure transactions while maintaining an intuitive flow across multiple features was critical. By integrating modern design principles, multilingual support, and seamless navigation, SALT delivers a rewarding user experience that’s functional, inclusive, and delightful.

Our Process

Pain Points

Fragmented Financial & Lifestyle Management:

Users often need multiple apps to manage salaries, transfer funds, and book lifestyle services like travel, leading to frustration and inefficiency.

Limited Accessibility & Language Support:

Many salary platforms lack multilingual interfaces and inclusivity, making it harder for a global, diverse audience to use them effectively.

Complex & Rigid User Flows:

Traditional apps have confusing layouts and poor navigation, making it difficult for users to complete simple tasks like booking a flight or sending money.

User Research:

User Research:

Conducted interviews with salaried employees, expatriates, and professionals to understand how they manage income, transfers, and lifestyle expenses.

User Journey Mapping:

Mapped workflows like “salary deposit & usage,” “sending money home,” and “booking travel with wallet funds” to uncover pain points in speed, trust, and convenience.

Prototyping and Testing:

Developed and tested low-to-high fidelity prototypes with diverse user groups to validate security flows, payment clarity, and overall usability across multiple languages.

Implementation

Unified Salary & Lifestyle Platform:

Designed an integrated experience combining salary wallet, card management, fund transfers, and travel booking in one seamless ecosystem.

Customizable Reports:

Built tools to generate, schedule, and export stakeholder-specific reports in formats like PDF, Excel, and CSV.

Configurable Alerts:

Developed a smart notification system with threshold-based alerts categorized by priority and delivered via email, SMS, or in-app.

Outcomes

The SALT app transformed how users manage their finances by making the money-tracking process simple, transparent, and stress-free. Through seamless onboarding, real-time tracking, and a clutter-free interface, it empowered users—from young professionals to experienced earners—to understand, plan, and control their finances effortlessly. The user-centric design reduced complexity and built trust, encouraging consistent financial awareness in daily life. Integrated insights, smart categorization, and personalized recommendations fostered deeper engagement. As a result, SALT not only improved users’ financial literacy and confidence but also helped them develop sustainable money-management habits—making finances feel clear, actionable, and within control.

Impact

SALT made a meaningful impact on how users interact with their money, helping them track and manage expenses with greater clarity and confidence. It lowered the barriers to financial planning by offering an intuitive, distraction-free experience users could rely on daily. With seamless expense tracking, actionable insights, and personalized guidance, users became more intentional and proactive with their finances. The platform fostered habits of mindful spending and continuous financial reflection, leading to improved money management and goal achievement. By promoting clarity, trust, and empowerment, SALT contributed to a more informed and financially confident user base.
