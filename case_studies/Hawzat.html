Hawzat

Where Cricket Meets Technology.

Project Summary

Howzat.ae was born from a simple yet powerful idea—making cricket more accessible, exciting, and intelligent for everyone, anytime, anywhere

Launched at BurJuman Mall, Dubai, Howzat is the world’s first AI-powered cage cricket platform, combining computer vision and augmented reality to deliver a groundbreaking, immersive experience. Players of all levels—friends, enthusiasts, and weekend warriors—can book a match effortlessly through a seamless online platform, then step into a high-tech arena where every shot, run, and reaction is tracked in real-time. With gamified competition, performance analytics, and a social, fast-paced environment, Howzat transforms casual matches into next-level gameplay.

More than just a booking site, Howzat is a gateway to a smarter, more connected cricket experience—where technology meets passion, and every game becomes a moment to remember.

Services

Strategy, Brand Design, Web design, Graphic Design

The Goal

  • Create a dynamic and recognizable logo and visual language
  • Design a responsive, user-friendly website to showcase the experience and enable bookings.
  • Build a sense of community and competition through visual storytelling and interface design

The Chalanges

Howzat.ae launched as the world’s first AI-powered cage cricket platform, introducing advanced computer vision, AR-powered gameplay, and real-time performance tracking. The challenge was to:

  • Build a memorable brand identity that reflects the energy and innovation of Howzat.
  • Design a website that communicates the concept quickly, builds trust, and converts visitors into players.
  • Ensure the platform feels both sporty and tech-forward, appealing to casual cricket lovers and early tech adopters alike.

Brand & logo Design

Howzat uses two distinct marks to represent the brand an Elemental icon and a Full Word logo. These marks are used independently of one another. Both marks have major and minor variations. The major variations should be used when the Howzat brand is the primary focus. The minor variations should be used as "background" elements, as a progression after the major variations, when client branding is visible, or when other prominent elements are present. This version is used when the Howzat brand is the primary focus. It is a bold, impactful design featuring a vibrant yellow "H" with a clearly defined cricket ball. This version features the name "HOWZAT" in a sharp, angled, almost graffiti-like font, conveying energy and excitement. It is used when the name "Howzat" needs to be explicitly presented, especially in larger applications or the first time the brand is introduced if the name isn't otherwise present.
