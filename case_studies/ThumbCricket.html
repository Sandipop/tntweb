Project Summary

Thumb cricket was born from a simple yet powerful idea—making cricket more accessible, exciting, and intelligent for everyone, anytime, anywhere

Launched at BurJuman Mall, Dubai, Howzat is the world’s first AI-powered cage cricket platform, combining computer vision and augmented reality to deliver a groundbreaking, immersive experience. Players of all levels—friends, enthusiasts, and weekend warriors—can book a match effortlessly through a seamless online platform, then step into a high-tech arena where every shot, run, and reaction is tracked in real-time. With gamified competition, performance analytics, and a social, fast-paced environment, Howzat transforms casual matches into next-level gameplay.

More than just a booking site, Howzat is a gateway to a smarter, more connected cricket experience—where technology meets passion, and every game becomes a moment to remember.
